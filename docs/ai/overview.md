MistAI is a specialized assistant trained on AOT:R data to provide instant answers to game-related questions. Instead of searching through long wikis, you can get direct advice on builds, items, and strategy.

### What can MistAI do?
* **Mechanics Guide:** Explains complex systems like Shifting, Prestige, and Memories.
* **Build Optimization:** Recommends Families and Serums for specific roles (e.g., Solo Bossing vs. Objective Defense).
* **Trading Help:** Provides current market estimates for high-value items based on recent game data.

### Beta Status
MistAI is currently in **Private Beta**. It is locked to the Support Server to allow the development team to monitor accuracy and performance before a global rollout.
